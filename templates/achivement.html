ACHIVENENTS

IIPC

Industry Institute Partnership Cell (IIPC) was established at Ideal Institute of Technology (an Institution of Ideal Group of Institutions), on February 24, 2009, with full financial support of All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), MHRD, Govt. of India. This Cell was established to bridge the gap between expectations of Industry and engineers graduating from this Institution.

The mission of IIPC at Ideal Institute of Technology is to develop close link between Industry and Institute, to focus on present day requirements of Professionals (Engineers / Technologists / Managers), meeting the future Human Resource needs and bringing about improvement in the performance of Industries by disseminating technical advances through informal educational techniques and thus effectively implementing R&D; and consultancy activities.

To fulfill the mission, IIPC takes special pains to organize national conferences, workshops, seminars, guest lectures, training programs and Industrial visits. It facilitates the process of providing consultancy services to industries and business organizations.

EDC

An Entrepreneurship Development Cell (EDC) exists at Ideal Institute of Technology, with the primary objective of developing entrepreneurial skills in the students and make them self dependent. The Cell fosters better linkages between the Institute, Industries and R&D; Institutions in the region and other related organizations, engaged in promoting small and medium enterprises.

Keeping the above objectives in view, the Entrepreneurship Development Cell plans to organize Entrepreneurship Awareness Camps for Idealities every year. Expert spreads awareness on all aspects of setting up an enterprise planned in collaboration with the National Small Scale Industry Corporation (NSIC), Okhla, New Delhi, District Industry Centers (DICs), National Bank for Agricultural and Rural Development (NABARD) and Small Scale Industry (SSI).

The Cell has received a grant-in-aid of Rs. 8,00,000/- (Rs. Eight Lacs Only) in March 2011 from All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India under the EDC Scheme.

NBA

The Flagship Institute of Ideal Group of Institutions, Ideal Institute of Technology has got accreditation from National Board of Accreditation, New Delhi for B.Tech. (CSE,ECE,EEE,ME,IT) Programme.


UPTU Academic Excellence Award

Ideal Institute of Technology received UPTU Academic Excellence award from the then Honorable Governor of UP Mr. T.V. Rajeswar on 13th June, 2008 for academic excellence.
