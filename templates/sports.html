SPOERTS

The Ideal Group of Institutions has a well organised sports committee and a sports complex. The entire Sports Complex is spread in an area of 10 Acres. The Sports Complex has one Multipurpose Hall for Indoor games i.e. Badminton, Table-Tennis, Carom board, Chess, Multi-Gym, Yoga , One cemented Cricket pitch, one ground each for Football, Cricket and volleyball, one cemented Basketball court and one Lawn Tennis Hard court.

For Colleges students and Staff Members, the timings for sports activities are 6 to 8 in the morning and 4 to 7 in the evening. The Sports Equipments / kits are issued to students during tournaments. Inter-Branch Tournaments are conducted in various games annually i.e. Chess, Football, Badminton, Table Tennis, Cross-Country Race, Volleyball shooting & smashing, Cricket.
