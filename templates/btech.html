B. Tech Admission Open 2022-2023

Ideal Institute of Technology, Ghaziabad offers a 4 year full time Programme in B. Tech with various branches as follows :

  • Computer Science and Engg.(CSE) with 90 seats
  • Civil Engg.(CE) with 90 seats
  • Mechanical Engg.(ME) with 90 seats
  • Electronics and Communications .(ECE) with 90 seats
  • Information Technology (IT) with 30 seats
  • Electrical and Electronics Engg. (EEE) with 30 seats

All programs are approved by AICTE and affiliated to Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University, Lucknow.
