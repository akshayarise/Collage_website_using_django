WELCOME TO IDEAL GROUP OF INSTITUTIONS

The “Ideal Group of Institutions, Ghaziabad” was started with the mission of imparting quality education in the year 1998. Ever since its humble beginnings in 1998, the Group has gone from strength to strength. It is now acknowledged as one of the leading institutions in the galaxy of Engineering Institutions in North India.

The Group has the dual privilege of being close to the industrial belt as well as the IT hub of NCR. The following institutes exist under the aegis of IDEAL GROUP :
