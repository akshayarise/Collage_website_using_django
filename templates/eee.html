Welcome To Deparment Of Electrical And Electronics Engineering

HOD's Message

As the technology is changing day by day and world is tending to automization in every field. The Electrical and Electronics Engineering is dynamic field of engineering, it deals with the generation, transmission, distribution and utilization of the electricity. This branch of engineering is an application of laws of the physics related to the electricity. The students got industrial exposure and trained to update their knowledge by seminars on recent trends, guest lectures and interactive session with eminent industrialists. The electrical and electronics engineering provides a kick start career in the field of design, development, production and installation. Electrical engineering is an exciting job where an engineer works on the cutting-edge technology.

About Department

Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering has experienced and highly qualified faculty members from IIT’s, NITS, Central and state government universities. All faculty members and staff provide best technical knowledge to the students as per the curriculum provided by the university, to fill the technical requirement of the students. The Electrical and Electronics Engineering department has established a firm goal to provide a high-quality engineering education to its students. The faculty guides the students for different projects and motivates them to publish the research paper. The computer lab always remains open during the college timings with internet facility so that students could use the internet according to their requirement. The department conducts training program of Auto CAD, Robotics, embedded system, MAT lab, simulink and Pspice software, the seminar program are organized for the students by renowned industrialists on different topics.
