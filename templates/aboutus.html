About Us

The “Ideal Group of Institutions, Ghaziabad” was started with the mission of imparting quality education in the year 1998. Ever since its humble beginning in 1998, the Group has gone from strength to strength. It is now acknowledged as one of the leading institutions in the galaxy of Engineering Institutions in North India.

The Group has the dual privilege of being close to the industrial belt as well as the IT hub of the NCR.The following institutes exist under the aegis of IDEAL GROUP :
1. Ideal Institute of Technology (1998)
(Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University, Uttar Pradesh, Lucknow Code : 028)
2. Ideal Institute of Management and Technology (2010)
(Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University, Uttar Pradesh, Lucknow Code : 645)

All the Institutions are affiliated to Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University, Lucknow and approved by AICTE. Ideal Institute of Management and Technology offers Polytechnic which is affiliated from Board of Technical Education, Lucknow. The Ideal Group of Institutions, Ghaziabad, is one of the continuously progressing Groups that always innovate to fulfill the dreams and aspirations of the students.
