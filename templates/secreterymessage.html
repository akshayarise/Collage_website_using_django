SECRETARY'S MESSAGE

At the outset, I extend a cordial welcome to all of you at Ideal Group of Institutions. The well known Ideal Group has taken the initiative of establishing a centre of excellence for Engineering, Architecture , Management and Professional Education.

The Group has grown in all aspects and has become an excellent academic body because of the commitment and dedication shown by the faculty and students over the years.

At Ideal, the students will get an opportunity to reactivate their nascent knowledge and skills in order to uplift academics, research and professionalism as a whole to elevate mankind. With this mission, we shall embark on our voyage to success.

“I hope, the students at Ideal Group will adhere to the paths we have paved and bring laurels to the ongoing legacy.”.


Gyan Prakash Goel
(Secretary)
