About Department

Electronics and Communication Engineering department is running with a vision to be recognized by the society as a department offering higher quality and research focus technical education. Department provides value based education to the students to face the challenges in electronics and communication field. Departmental goals:

  • Continuously strive to improve result and employability
  • Upgrade instructional, laboratory and library facilities
  • Expose students to recent advances by motivating them to participate in seminars, workshops and talks by guest speakers
  • Develop faculty through qualification enhancement schemes and industrial exposure
  • Regularly council students to develop desired skills, competencies and attitude.
  • Develop facilities for starting PG courses and research in the department.
